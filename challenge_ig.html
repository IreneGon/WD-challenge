Discovering Charlottenburg - Berlin

One of my favourite areas to eat out in Berlin is Charlottenburg.
This elegant and cosmopolitan area is full of excellent choices for all tastes and flavours.


#My picks

  1. French
  2. Vietnamese
  3. Japanese
  4. Thai

French

A Michelin-listed "bistro" that provides excellent ambient, staff, food, everything! Lamazere

Vietnamese

If you are more Eastern-bound, Kantstraße offers excellent Vietnamese restaurants such as Madame Ngo

Japanese

Another iconic restaurants is Kuchi, as well as the Michelin awarded Ryotei 893.
